Democracy vs. Disinformation

Don't let rumors cloud your civic duty. Get the facts.

cancel MYTH

"The voting machines can be remotely hacked via Wi-Fi or Bluetooth."

check_circle FACT

EVMs are standalone machines with no wireless or network capabilities. They are physically secure and undergo rigorous multi-level testing.

cancel MYTH

"If I don't have a Voter ID card, I cannot vote at all."

check_circle FACT

If your name is in the electoral roll, you can vote using any of the 12 alternative photo ID documents prescribed by the ECI, like Aadhaar or PAN card.
